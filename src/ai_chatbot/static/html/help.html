Chatbot - Help Guide/Tutorial

Features

  • Gets train ticket information, single or returns
  • Train delay prediction
  • Voice recognition (speech-to-text) and synthesis (text-to-speech)

Example scenario: Getting train ticket

  • You: I want a train ticket from Norwich to London Liverpool Street
  • Bot: What date are you traveling on?
  • You: 12th of Feburary 2020
  • Bot: What time do you want to leave at?
  • You: 15:15
  • Bot: you said: norwich-nrw to luton-lut on the 12 feburary 2020 at 15:15 - Is that correct?
  • You: Yes

Train delay prediction

This chatbot can also predict train delay also. For example:

"Can you please let me know the delay on my train from Norwich that was supposed to leave at 1pm, and left at 1:20pm, going to London Liverpool Street to arrive at 3pm, and we just passed Colchester at 2:30pm."

You will need to put the following:

  • The departure station name
  • The departure station's supposed to leave time (planned time)
  • The departure station's actually left time (actual time)
  • The arrival station name
  • The arrival station's supposed to arrival time (planned time)
  • The station you just passed name
  • The departure of the station you just passed (actual time)

Voice recognition and synthesis

You need Chrome/Chromium in order to use voice recognition, voice synthesis is supported by most major browsers. You can disable/enable both recognition and synthesis features with the two buttons ("Start/Stop Voice Recognition" and "Enable/Disable text to speech") below the text input.
